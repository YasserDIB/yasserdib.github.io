About Bellabooty

Bellabooty's founding team is a group of friends consisting of personal trainers and a product engineer. Being personal trainers and working with a wide variety of clients across many different fitness levels, we saw the need to build stronger glutes in every single one of our clients…

With one of the best movements for growing stronger glutes being hip thrusts, it became a must-have in our workouts. However, there were always two issues that came alongside hip thrusts… the need for the barbell, and the pain caused by it. Like many other people who saw the benefits of hip thrusts but ran into these same problems, we tried all the solutions out there, but none actually solved the issues. So our team went to work on creating a better solution and after many different prototypes and iterations, we created the Bellabooty Belt…
